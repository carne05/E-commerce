About us

AURÉA was born from a singular, radiant vision: to capture the shimmering magic of dawn’s first light and transform it into color for your face. Founded by a collective of artists and visionaries, we believed makeup should be more than a routine—it should be a ritual of light. We traveled from windswept cliffs to hidden gardens to find the purest earth-born pigments, blending timeless color artistry with a modern, minimalist soul. AURÉA isn’t just about beauty; it’s about that luminous moment where nature meets expression. Welcome to the golden hour, every single day.
